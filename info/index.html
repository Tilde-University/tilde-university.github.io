The heads of the project are Asad Chishti, Headmaster (of local students), and Erin Kennedy, Headmistress (of remote students).

We’re attempting to build a community on the original slogan of the World Wide Web:

Share what you know.

If there was a symbol for this community it would be the ‘?’ to represent curiousity. The first class begins with a fairly strong computational background but we're quite multi-faceted and hoping to sprout in all sorts of directions.
